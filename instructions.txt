Pancake
1. Mix 1 cup of flour, 1 cup of milk, and 1 egg in a bowl.
2. Add 1 tablespoon of sugar and a pinch of salt. Stir well.
3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
4. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
5. Serve warm with syrup or fruits.
